Get ready for a high-stakes art heist with a heartwarming family twist as Stanley Tucci stars in "Masterplan," premiering exclusively on Prime Video this October.

Stanley Tucci, renowned for his versatile performances, is set to captivate audiences once again in "Masterplan," an original heist film coming exclusively to Prime Video on October 16. This highly anticipated Amazon production promises a thrilling caper centered around one of the world's most iconic artworks, infused with an unexpected and compelling family dynamic. Prepare for a film that blends high-tension thrills with genuine emotional depth, all delivered by a stellar cast and creative team.

## The Audacious Plot: Stealing the Mona Lisa

At the heart of "Masterplan" is an audacious plot to steal the Mona Lisa, a feat that would undoubtedly go down in history. Stanley Tucci takes on the role of a legendary master thief, a character whose cunning and experience are unmatched. However, his latest, most ambitious plan requires more than just his singular brilliance; it demands a team.

The twist that elevates "Masterplan" beyond a typical heist film lies in the composition of this team. Tucci's character enlists two young strangers, a brilliant cybercrime expert and a skilled explosives specialist, to assist him. As the intricate plan unfolds, they discover a shocking truth: these two individuals are his long-lost children. This revelation throws an emotional curveball into an already high-stakes operation, forcing an unexpected family reunion where trust must be built under immense pressure. ## A Family Affair: Overcoming Differences for the Ultimate Score

The familial twist is not just a narrative device; it's the core engine driving the film's conflict and charm. Thrown together by circumstance and a shared, dangerous goal, this newly formed family must navigate their past, present, and future while executing an impossible heist. They must learn to cooperate, overcome personal differences, and reconcile years of separation—all while outsmarting authorities and securing the world's most famous painting. This dynamic promises a blend of thrilling action, comedic moments, and heartfelt drama, as the characters struggle with both the technicalities of the heist and the complexities of their unexpected bond.

Co-starring alongside Tucci are Simona Tabasco and Victor Belmondo, who bring their talents to round out this intriguing ensemble. Their performances are crucial in establishing the chemistry and tension necessary for such a unique family unit.

## Behind the Scenes: A Visionary Creative Team and Global Production

"Masterplan" benefits from a strong creative team guiding its vision. The film is directed by Thomas Vincent, known for his adept handling of suspense and character-driven narratives. The screenplay was penned by BAFTA-winning David Wolstencroft, ensuring a script that is both intelligent and engaging.

The production itself is an international collaboration, a co-production between Gaumont and Amazon MGM Studios. This partnership has allowed for a grand scale, with filming taking place across picturesque locations in France and Italy. Such a global approach not only adds authenticity to the European setting of the heist but also underscores the ambitious scope of the film.
