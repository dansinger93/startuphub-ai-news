In today's fast-paced digital landscape, businesses are constantly seeking innovative ways to scale operations and enhance customer engagement. RingCentral, a leading provider of global enterprise cloud communications, has emerged as a prime example of how generative AI can be a transformative force, particularly in the realm of customer program management. By strategically integrating ChatGPT Work, RingCentral has not only streamlined its internal processes but also achieved remarkable growth in its customer access initiatives.

RingCentral's journey demonstrates the profound impact of AI on overcoming common business challenges, transforming fragmented data into a unified, actionable system, and empowering teams to achieve more with fewer resources.

## The Challenge: Navigating Data Silos and Manual Processes

Before the integration of advanced AI, RingCentral, like many large organizations, grappled with significant data management hurdles. Vaneet Seth, Senior Manager of R&D Efficiency and PMO at RingCentral, highlighted how his role, which involves orchestrating various cross-functional stakeholders and initiatives, was historically bogged down by data silos. Information was scattered across disparate systems, making it incredibly difficult to gain a holistic view of program progress, identify potential risks, or ensure consistent alignment across diverse teams.

This fragmented approach led to a manual, time-consuming process that was inherently unscalable. Tracking program milestones, gathering insights, and making informed decisions became an uphill battle, often resulting in delays and inefficiencies that hampered customer program expansion.

## ChatGPT Work: A Catalyst for Unified Data and Agile Execution

The pivotal shift for RingCentral came with the implementation of ChatGPT Work. This integration didn't just add another tool to their stack; it fundamentally changed how data was managed and utilized. Seth describes the outcome as creating a "living, breathing operating system" for their program management. Instead of static data residing in isolated pockets, raw information is now actively processed, synthesized, and transformed into real-time, actionable intelligence.

This AI-powered transformation has allowed RingCentral to move beyond reactive reporting to proactive management. The system intelligently aggregates information from various sources, including status updates, owner notes, due diligence findings, and risk assessments. This synthesized data then feeds directly into decision-making processes, ensuring that information is always current, gaps are identified early, and the necessary context for strategic choices is preserved.

## Exponential Growth: Scaling Customer Programs from 6 to 80

One of the most compelling testaments to ChatGPT Work's effectiveness is the dramatic scaling of RingCentral's customer access program. Seth proudly shared that the AI solution helped him expand the program "from 6 customers to 80." This staggering 1200% growth, moving from a nascent pilot phase to serving a robust customer base, underscores the profound efficiency gains and increased capacity enabled by the AI integration.

What makes this achievement even more remarkable is that this expansion occurred while Seth was essentially a "team of one" managing the program. ChatGPT Work acted as a powerful assistant, effectively multiplying his capacity and allowing him to manage a significantly larger workload and a greater number of customer engagements. This kind of operational efficiency is a game-changer for businesses looking to expand without proportional increases in headcount, much like how other companies are leveraging AI to scale their go-to-market efforts or generate substantial pipeline figures. For example, similar AI applications have helped [Nvidia scale GTM with ChatGPT work](https://www.startuphub.ai/ai-news/artificial-intelligence/2026/nvidia-scales-gtm-with-chatgpt-work), and [Zapier uses ChatGPT to generate seven figures](https://www.startuphub.ai/ai-news/artificial-intelligence/2026/zapier-uses-chatgpt-to-generate-seven-figures-in-pipeline) in pipeline.

## The PMO's Evolution: From Oversight to Strategic Partnership

The integration of ChatGPT Work also redefined the role of RingCentral's Project Management Office (PMO). Traditionally, PMOs often focus on oversight, reviewing plans, and compiling status reports. With AI capabilities, RingCentral's PMO has transitioned into an active participant in the execution phase.

The AI assists in analyzing complex data, identifying potential bottlenecks, and ensuring projects stay on track, transforming the PMO from a reactive reporting function to a proactive strategic partner. This dynamic and responsive project management approach allows for faster adaptation and more informed interventions, directly contributing to the success of customer initiatives. ## Key Takeaways for Businesses

RingCentral's success story with ChatGPT Work offers valuable lessons for any organization looking to optimize its operations and customer programs:

1.  **AI for Data Unification:** Generative AI can effectively break down data silos, creating a unified and "living" data system that provides real-time insights.
2.  **Scalability Without Linear Growth:** AI empowers smaller teams or even individuals to manage significantly larger workloads, enabling exponential growth in programs and customer reach without a corresponding increase in human resources.
3.  **Proactive Management:** AI shifts the focus from reactive reporting to proactive execution, allowing teams to identify risks, gaps, and opportunities much earlier.
4.  **Strategic Empowerment:** AI transforms support functions like PMOs into strategic partners, capable of contributing directly to project success through advanced data synthesis and actionable intelligence.

RingCentral's experience is a compelling demonstration of how AI, when strategically applied, can be a powerful engine for efficiency, growth, and enhanced customer engagement in the modern enterprise.
